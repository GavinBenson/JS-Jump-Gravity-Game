About me

My grandpa taught me sand business, and his grand-pappy taught him fore'. My history can be drawn back for thousands of years of prospective sand business-men. I aim with my sand business to continue on their legacy and provide the essential exotic sands that many can't live without. I buy my exotic sands from locations all around the world including but not limited to Sierra Leone, Sudan, Somalia, Vietnam, Iraq, Syria, and Antarctica. We ship our sands by the grain in specialized spaceships which melt the sand to over 375,000 Kelvin before shooting the sand attached to giant repurposed tungsten rods weighing approximately 3.4 million tons right to your doorstep.
